Under the sun, on a dusty ground,
Where dreams in silence slowly are found,
A bat is raised, the bowler runs,
A story begins with countless suns.

The pitch lies calm, yet holds the fight,
Between hope and fear in morning light,
A spinning ball, a swinging seam,
Turns reality into a dream.

The crowd erupts with every cheer,
In every heart, both joy and fear,
A boundary flies, the fielders chase,
Time itself slows in this sacred space.

From gully cricket in narrow lanes,
To roaring crowds and global games,
It binds us all, both old and young,
A universal, unspoken tongue.

A captain’s call, a daring play,
A match that changes in one over’s sway,
Heroes rise, legends are made,
In every shot that a batter has played.

Win or lose, the spirit stays,
In sweaty shirts and sunlit days,
For cricket is more than just a game—
It’s passion, pride, and a burning flame. 🏏🔥
